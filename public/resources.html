Are you a newbie to wardrobe management?


No worries, we have some handy resources to get you started.

The following are some helpful guides about creating and maintaining a wardrobe...



Wardrobe Basics Checklist

Use this checklist from Real Simple magazine to build a strong foundation of timeless pieces for a woman's wardrobe.


How to Build a Manly Wardrobe: Part 1

A guide with lists from the Art of Manliness' website on how to develop a men's wardrobe based on general career type.


How to Start a Capsule Wardrobe: a Guide for Beginners

Lindsay from the Pinch of Yum blog has a nice simple guide to starting a capsule wardrobe.


How to Build a Capsule Wardrobe: a Beginners Guide

Another approach to the capsule wardrobe. This one breaks things down into a 5 week process with weeks 1 -3 dedicated to organizing/decluttering. From Sophie of the Adventures of a Creative Girl blog/online community.


17 Invaluable Tips for Anybody with Too Many Clothes

A visual (and cheeky) set of tips on cleaning out your closet from Buzzfeed.


How to Finally Clean Out Your Closet for Good

Courtney Carver from Be More With Less breaks down closet cleaning into a clear step-by-step process.
